Privacy Policy for Scene Cards

Last updated: April 2026

Scene Cards is developed by Keith Tunney.

Data Collection

Scene Cards does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. The developer has no access to any information you enter or store in the app.

Your Files

All documents, images, and project files you create or import in Scene Cards are stored locally on your device or in your personal iCloud Drive account. Files are never uploaded to any server operated by the developer.

Third-Party Services

Scene Cards does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Camera and Photo Library

If you use the camera or photo import features, images are saved directly to your project files on your device or iCloud. They are not transmitted anywhere.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL.

Contact

For questions about this policy, please use the support page.
